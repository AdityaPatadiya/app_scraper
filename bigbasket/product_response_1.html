Aashirvaad Atta/Godihittu - Whole Wheat

About the Product

Aashirvaad Whole Wheat Atta is a premium, all-natural flour that has become the number one choice for health-conscious consumers across India. Sourced directly from rural Indian farmers, this 100% pure whole wheat atta is made with the utmost care and attention to detail. The wheat grains are showered with the perfect balance of rain and sun, resulting in a heavy, amber-coloured flour that is rich in natural dietary fibres and nutrients. Through a meticulous 3-step cleaning process and traditional 'chakki' grinding method, Aashirvaad ensures the retention of the wheat's highest nutritional value. The dough made from this superior atta absorbs more water, creating soft, fluffy rotis that stay fresh for longer. Enjoy the benefits of a protein-rich, fibre-packed whole wheat flour that supports easy digestion and overall well-being for you and your family.
